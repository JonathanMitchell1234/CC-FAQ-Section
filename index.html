FAQ

I don't own a computer. Will Commonwealth Coders provide that?
Absolutely! All students will be provided with an M1 Macbook Pro to borrow for the duration of the class.
What hours will be dedicated to class time?
Commonwealth Coders classes begin at 8:30AM Central Time and 9:30AM Eastern Time. Class ends most days at approximately 3:00PM Central and 4:00PM Eastern. All major Holidays will be off days.
Do I need any prior experience with coding or computer expertise?
No! Commonwealth Coders is designed for students of all experience levels to be able to successfully gain employment as a Jr. Web Developer. However, strong Algebra skills as well as a strong work-ethic are greatly encouraged!
Am I guaranteed to get a job in Web Development after this class?
Commonwealth Coders will provide you with the skills and education necessary to obtain employment, However, what you get out of the class is up to you. Job opportunities are posted frequently in the class Slack channel.
What can I do to prepare for class?
There are many resources available online if you'd like to get a head start before classes begin. Some excellent resources include but are not limited to: FreeCodeCamp, W3schools, CodePen, Code Academy, and Udemy.
